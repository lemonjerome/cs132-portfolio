Hi. We are GRACE.

And this is our project, How Can the Philippines Catch Up?: An Analysis of World University Rankings. This study explores non-education-related factors that influence a country's standing. In addition to examining the annual progression of global university standings, we analyze social and economic indicators. The insights gained from the data are then used to propose strategies for improving the Philippines' position on the global stage.

Here's an overview of our project.

Motivation

Recent news about PH university rankings are always negative, either about a lower yearly performance or a ranking where we placed near the bottom.

Problem

The government prioritizes educational programs that yield minimal results or bring insignificant changes, often unnoticed by students, teachers, and parents.

Solution

Looking into social and economic factors that are not directly connected to education may be a good starting point and guide in creating future programs.

Hypothesis

Countries with high-performing economies and stable societies perform better in global university rankings.
